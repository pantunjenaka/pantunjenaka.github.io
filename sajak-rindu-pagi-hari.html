Dia perempuan membawa buku setiap hari 4. Dia laki-laki meminjam uang setiap hari 5. Kami mencari Toni setiap hari 6. Dia laki-laki merindukan dia perempuan setiap hari 7. Dia perempuan merindukan dia laki- laki setiap hari 8. Mereka merindukan saya setiap hari 9. Kita merindukan mereka setiap hari 10. Kamu merindukan kami setiap hari. ​


Jawaban:

1. Active: I miss you every day.

Passive: You are missed by me every day.

2. Active: They miss you every day.

Passive: You are missed by them every day.

3. Active: She brings the book every day.

Passive: The book is brought by her every day.

4. Active: He borrows money every day.

Passive: Money is borrowed by him every day.

5. Active: We search Toni every day.

Passive: Tony is searched by us every day.

6. Active: He misses her every day.

Passive: She is missed by him every day.

7. Active: She misses him every day.

Passive:  He is missed by her every day.

8. Active: They miss me every day.

Passive: I am missed by them every day.

9. Active: We miss them every day.

Passive: They are missed by us every day.

10. Active: You miss us every day.

Passive: We are missed by you every day.

Penjelasan:

Dalam pembentukan kalimat passive dalam bentuk present tense, pastikan subjek kalimatnyabenar, dan to be nya sesuai dengan subjek tersebut.

Kalau I harus pakai am, you harus pakai are, kalau subjeknya jamak harus pakai are jangan is, dst.

Pastikan juga bahwa subjek kalimat aktif adalah yang tadinya adalah objek di kalimat aktif, dan pronoun nya harus menyesuaikan dari tadinya dia object pronoun (misalnya me, you, him, her, them, us) menjadi subject pronoun (I, you, He, she, we, they) karena memang posisinya sudha berubah jadi subjek kalimat.
