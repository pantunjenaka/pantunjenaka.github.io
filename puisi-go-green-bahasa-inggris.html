19. deskripsi tentang Green house dalam bahasa Inggris


A greenhouse (also called a glasshouse, or, if with sufficient heating, a hothouse) is a structure with walls and roof made chiefly of transparent material, such as glass, in which plants requiring regulated climatic conditions are grown.[3] These structures range in size from small sheds to industrial-sized buildings. A miniature greenhouse is known as a cold frame. The interior of a greenhouse exposed to sunlight becomes significantly warmer than the external ambient temperature, protecting its contents in cold weather. Many commercial glass greenhouses or hothouses are high tech production facilities for vegetables or flowers. The glass greenhouses are filled with equipment including screening installations, heating, cooling, lighting, and may be controlled by a computer to optimise conditions for plant growth.

27. deskripsi tentang green house dalam bahasa inggris


The greenhouse effect is the natural process by which the atmosphere traps some of the Sun's energy, warming the Earth enough to support life.
Most mainstream scientists believe a human-driven increase in "greenhouse gases" is increasing the effect artificially. These gases include carbon dioxide, emitted by fossil fuel burning and deforestation, and methane, released from rice paddies and landfill sites.



Efek rumah kaca adalah proses alami dimana atmosfir perangkap sebagian energi matahari, pemanasan Bumi cukup untuk mendukung kehidupan.

38. Buatlah tentang GO GREEN dalam Bahasa inggris. paling banyak 6 paragraf


GREENING is one of the important activities that must be implemented conceptually in handling environmental crises. It is so important that reforestation is already a national program implemented throughout Indonesia. Many facts indicate that not infrequently the construction is built on agricultural land and green open space. Though plants in the ecosystems act as the first producer to convert solar energy into potential energy for other creatures and convert CO2 to O2 in the process of photosynthesis. So by increasing greening in urban areas means to reduce CO2 or other pollutants that contribute to the occurrence of greenhouse effect or climate disturbance. In addition to vegetation plays a role in life and physical health of the environment, also aesthetic and mental health role. Given the important role of this vegetation, especially in urban areas to handle environmental crises, it is necessary to plan and plant vegetation for reforestation conceptually. From various observations and research there is a tendency that the implementation of greening is not conceptual, even impressed origin. Choose the type of plant for easy reason, cheap and fast growing. Urban greening Greening in the broadest sense is all the power to restore, maintain and improve the condition of the land in order to produce and function optimally, either as a water regulator or environmental protector. Some say that the greening of the city is an attempt to green the city by implementing the management of urban parks, environmental parks, green lanes and so on. In this case, urban greening is an activity of open space filling in urban areas. In the process of photosynthesis green plants take CO2 and remove C6H12O6 and the role of O2 is needed by living things. Therefore, the role of green plants is needed to capture CO2 and release O2 back into the air. In addition, various metabolic processes of green plants can provide various functions for the needs of living things that can improve the quality of the environment. Each year the vegetation on earth entails approximately 150,000 million tons of CO2 and 25,000 million tons of hydrogen by liberating 400,000 million tons of oxygen into the atmosphere, and producing 450,000 million tons of organic substances. Every hour 1 ha of green leaves absorb 8 kg CO2 which is equivalent to CO2 exhaled by human breath approximately 200 people in the same time. Each planted tree has an air-cooling capacity equal to an average of 5 air conditioners (AC), which is operated 20 hours continuously each day.

1. Do not throw litter

Littering is often done by many people. Though littering can increase global warming, such as taking out the trash in the river. What consequences? As a result of throwing garbage in the river was flooded. Well, that did not happen like that, do not litter.

2. Do not burn trash

Burning garbage is not a good way to get rid of trash. If we burn garbage, the smoke can damage the ozone layer. When the ozone layer damaged, ultraviolet rays can enter Earth excessive and its impact will affect the living beings.

3. Mamatikan lights or electronics when not needed

This can reduce the addition of electricity and increase the cost of electricity.
