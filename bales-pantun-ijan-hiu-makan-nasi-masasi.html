______________________________________

Soal 1

[tex] = 35\% \times 50[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{35}{100} \times 50[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{35}{10} \times 5[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{175}{10} [/tex]

[tex] = \boxed{17,5}[/tex]

Jadi hasilnya adalah 17,5

______________________________________

Soal 2

[tex] = \sqrt{144} + 7 {}^{2} \times \sqrt[3]{64} [/tex]

[tex] = 12 + 49 \times 4[/tex]

Dahulukan perkalian

[tex] = 12 + 196[/tex]

[tex] = \boxed{208}[/tex]

Jadi hasilnya adalah 208
